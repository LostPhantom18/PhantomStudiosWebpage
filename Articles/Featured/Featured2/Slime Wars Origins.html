Slime Wars: Origins

August 1st, 2024 4 min read

Early Gamer Days

During Elementary school, often we had computer lab time or computer time rewarded if our class was doing a good job/we got our work done. We were allowed to play Cool Math Games however some websites like NotDoppler were not blocked. NotDoppler wasn't a super bad website but it did have more graphic games for 8-12 year olds.

Exploring Web-based Games

As kids were finding new websites and finding new website games I came across Age of War while playing on a laptop at home. I played the game for hours, replaying, trying different strategies etc. I also came across a game called Stick Wars.

Slime Wars

Slime Wars will be a hybrid as well as a new entity. I want to take the ideas and create a new enjoyable game with more replayability. Im taking developing a little slower so I can make sure it has good game design before jumping in and making decisions. Key gameplay will be gathering resources, creating troops, planning, building important buildings and so much more. I would love to eventually have a PvP gamemode as well as different themes. But that is very low priority stuff.
