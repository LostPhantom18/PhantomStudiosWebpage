Who am I and what is Phantom Studios?

July 30, 2024 8 min read

My name is Jonah, a Game Design Student based in Canada. Since a young age I liked to figure out stuff. I liked to be handy and build stuff. Eventually that need to build stuff turned into other forms. I started playing roblox in rougly 2014 and found it really exciting. I loved the concept of playing other peoples creations. Soon afterwards around 2015-2015 I learned you could donwload Roblox Studio and anyone could make games! I starting learning how to use the build tools, how to control objects with scripts. Youtube was my go to. I would love to watch different youtubers teach me this new language. Which is Roblox Lua. I remember forcing my cousin to watch videos with me so he could become a game dev like me and we could try to get rich off of making robux and trading it in for currency. My issue was staying focused on a project as a 12-16 year old kid is not easy. A few years later, Im in fulltime game design and want to create my own stuff. I think it would be cool to be either a solo or indie dev. I hope to make that dream come true and produce good games. I want to playtest lots and not putout crap. With the communitys feedback once my games our ready for alpha. I hope to make games that are enjoyable to many.
